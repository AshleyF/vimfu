Tips

Click the terminal to focus, then type. Without a shell connected, keystrokes echo locally (CR → CRLF).

Use A−/A+ or Ctrl-−/Ctrl-+ to zoom; the grid auto-fits the pane. Resize the window and the rows/cols update.

then click Connect.
