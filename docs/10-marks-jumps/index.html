Marks Jumps

  1. Setting and Jumping to Marks — m{a-z} sets a mark. '{a-z} jumps to its line. `{a-z} jumps to its exact spot.
  2. Special Marks — Marks Vim sets for you automatically.
  3. Tick vs Backtick — ' jumps to the line of a mark. ` jumps to the exact position.
  4. The Jump List — Ctrl-O / Ctrl-I — walk back and forward through your big jumps.
  5. The Change List — g; / g, — walk through the places you've been editing.
