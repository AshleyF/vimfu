Screen Position Jumps

H, M, L — High, Middle, Low on the visible screen.

id: wider-motions.screen-positions · part: wider-motions · keys: H, M, L · lessons: 94

H jumps to the top of the visible screen, M to the middle, L to the bottom. They're motions to a screen position, not a file position — they don't scroll.

Three single-key jumps to positions on the current screen: top, middle, bottom. They don't scroll — they move the cursor to where you can already see.

Tip. H/M/L are paired with zt/zz/zb (which scroll the cursor's current line to the top/middle/bottom of the screen, see `scroll.center-top-bottom`). Together they let you reposition either the cursor or the viewport without thinking.

Reference

Key Lands on
H Top visible line
M Middle visible line
L Bottom visible line
{n}H {n} lines below top
{n}L {n} lines above bottom
