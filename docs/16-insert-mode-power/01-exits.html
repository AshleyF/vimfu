Exiting Insert Mode

Three ways out, and why people remap.

Keys: Esc, Ctrl-[, Ctrl-C

Esc, Ctrl-[, and Ctrl-C all exit insert mode — but they're not identical. Esc and Ctrl-[ trigger InsertLeave; Ctrl-C bypasses it.

Exiting insert mode

There are three ways out of insert mode. Two are equivalent, one is subtly different — and that subtle difference matters.

Tip. Many users remap Caps Lock → Ctrl at the OS level, then use Ctrl-[ as their exit. It's the single biggest ergonomic win for Vim.
