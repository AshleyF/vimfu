Survival

  1. Open, Save, Quit — Launch Neovim, write your work, and get out — three ways each
  2. Insert and Back Again — i to type, Esc to come home
  3. Undo and Redo — u to undo. Ctrl-R to redo. You cannot lose work.
  4. Move with hjkl — Left, down, up, right — without leaving home row
