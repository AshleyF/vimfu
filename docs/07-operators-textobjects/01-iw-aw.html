Inner Word vs A Word

iw and aw — the first text objects.

id: operators-textobjects.iw-aw · part: operators-textobjects · keys: iw, aw · lessons: 74

Text objects produce a (start, end) range without using the cursor. iw is the word the cursor is in. aw is that word plus surrounding whitespace.

Up to now, the noun in operator+motion has been a motion — a path from where you are to where you'll end up. Text objects change that. They name a chunk of text directly, no path required. The first one to learn is the word.

🔧 Under the Hood — i Means "Inner." a Means "Around."

Every text object comes in two flavors:

  • i{noun} — inner: the noun itself, without delimiters or surrounding whitespace.
  • a{noun} — around: the noun with its delimiters or trailing whitespace.

For a word (iw / aw), there are no delimiters — just whitespace. So:

  • iw = the keyword characters under the cursor.
  • aw = the word + the trailing space (or leading space if at end of line).

For a quoted string, i" is what's between the quotes; a" includes the quotes. For parens, i( is the contents; a( includes the parens themselves. Same i/a rule everywhere.

🔧 Under the Hood — Why Text Objects Aren't Motions

A motion takes you from the cursor to somewhere else. So dw deletes from the cursor forward to the start of the next word. The cursor's position matters.

A text object is cursor-position-tolerant: if the cursor is anywhere inside the chunk, the chunk is the chunk. diw on any character of foo deletes foo. You don't need to be at the start.

This is why text objects are easier on your fingers than motions for in-place edits. Combined with ciw for "change-this-word-no-matter-where-the-cursor-is," they're the workhorse of Vim editing.

Tip. Word objects use the same 'iskeyword' setting as w. So in foo->bar, ciw on foo changes just foo. WORD objects (iW, aW) treat anything-not-whitespace as the word — change the whole foo->bar.

Reference

Object Range
iw Word (keyword chars only)
aw Word + surrounding whitespace
iW WORD (whitespace boundaries)
aW WORD + surrounding whitespace
