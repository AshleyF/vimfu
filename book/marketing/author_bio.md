**Ashley Feniello** is a software engineer who has been living in Vim for a
quarter-century, with tens of thousands of lines of code at Microsoft and Intel
behind him. Along the way he's been Vim's quiet evangelist on every team he's
joined — the coworker patiently explaining why `cit` beats ten arrow keys, why
the dot command is the most powerful key on the keyboard, and why "I keep
getting stuck in some weird mode" is a five-minute conversation that changes
how someone edits forever. *VimFu* is the book that those conversations
finally became.
